    🔬 Subject Overview

    Plant Anatomy & Physiology

    Plant Anatomy covers the internal structure of roots, stems, leaves, and reproductive organs — tissue systems, vascular bundles, and secondary growth. Plant Physiology builds on this by examining how plants function: photosynthesis, respiration, transpiration, plant hormones, and responses to stress.

    🧬 Subject Overview

    Genetics, Taxonomy & Biotechnology

    Genetics covers inheritance patterns, chromosomal behavior, and molecular basis of heredity. Taxonomy deals with identification, nomenclature, and classification of plants into families and orders. Biotechnology applies tools like tissue culture and genetic engineering to crop improvement and conservation.
