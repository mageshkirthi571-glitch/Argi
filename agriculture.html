Core Topics

Agricultural Practices

🌱 Cultivation

Crop Cultivation Methods

Land preparation, seed selection, sowing methods (broadcasting, drilling, transplanting), spacing, and crop establishment techniques for field and horticultural crops.

🍃 Organic

Organic Farming

Use of compost, vermicompost, green manure, biofertilizers, and crop rotation to maintain fertility without synthetic chemical inputs. Emphasizes long-term soil and ecosystem health.

🪨 Soil

Soil Management

Soil testing, pH correction, organic matter addition, erosion control through contour bunding and mulching, and maintaining soil structure for healthy root growth.

💧 Water

Irrigation Techniques

Comparison of surface, drip, and sprinkler irrigation; scheduling based on crop water requirement; water-saving practices for water-scarce regions.

🐛 Protection

Pest & Disease Management

Identification of common pests and pathogens, Integrated Pest Management (IPM) combining biological control, resistant varieties, and judicious pesticide use.

🌾 Nutrition

Crop Nutrition

Role of macronutrients (N, P, K) and micronutrients in plant growth, deficiency symptoms, and balanced fertilizer application based on soil test recommendations.
