About this project

Botany & Agriculture, side by side

Botany is the scientific study of plants — their structure, growth, classification, and uses. Agriculture applies that knowledge to grow food, fibre, and other crops sustainably. This hub brings the two together, alongside forestry and conservation, in one student-curated resource.

Whether you're preparing for a B.Sc. Botany exam, planning an organic farm plot, identifying a plant on a nature walk, or studying for a Forest Guard exam — there's a corner here for you.

🌿 Did you know?

Taxonomy in a nutshell

Every plant has a unique two-part scientific name (genus + species) under the binomial system devised by Carl Linnaeus — a system still used in Botany Notes and the Plant ID database on this site.
