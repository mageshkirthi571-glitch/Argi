Select the leaf shape that best matches what you see, and get a shortlist of common tree species to compare against.

🍃 Leaf Shape

Compound / Pinnate

Leaflets arranged along a central stalk. Common in: Neem, Tamarind, Gulmohar, Drumstick.

🍃 Leaf Shape

Simple / Ovate

Single, broad, egg-shaped leaf. Common in: Banyan, Peepal, Mango, Guava.

🍃 Leaf Shape

Needle-like

Thin, narrow, needle-shaped leaves. Common in: Pine, Casuarina, Cypress.

Tip: Combine leaf shape with bark texture, flower color, and fruit type for accurate identification.
